About Us

We are Forged Interactive.
We take pride in our work, seeking innovation in video game design and development. Forged Interactive was officially founded under the name, Xepherfae Studios, on January 1st, 2021 with the release of a jam game titled Spardyr Reimagined: Detention Imminent, a tribute to the original title, Spardyr, by Andrew Stewart Productions. In 2022, we rebranded to Forged Interactive.
We have navigated through trial and error to reach our goals of bringing our players new and innovative titles, finally reaching the long-term goal of releasing our first commercial title, To Hell and Back, on Steam.
